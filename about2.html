Our History

JELFAD began with a simple goal: to make fueling easy, fair, and reliable. From our earliest outlet to our expanding footprint, we’ve kept our promise of transparent pricing, clean stations, and friendly service.

As we grew, we invested in storage, logistics, and quality assurance to keep products available and standards high. Today, our network spans multiple cities with 24/7 select branches and support for retail, corporate, and industrial customers.
